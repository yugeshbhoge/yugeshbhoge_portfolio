Eco Coach

IoT-based Carbon Footprint Tracker & Sponsorship Success.

Innovation & Management

Eco Coach is an IoT device designed to monitor vehicle emissions in real-time, helping drivers understand and reduce their carbon footprint.

Technical Scope

Developed the hardware prototype using microcontrollers and emission sensors to log data to a cloud dashboard.

Project Management

Successfully negotiated with institute administration for a budget of ~Rs 2.5 Lakhs and secured external industrial sponsorship.
